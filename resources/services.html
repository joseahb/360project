What is Off-plan sales?

Off-plan sales is simply selling your new homes before they have been fully constructed. It’s great for both the residential developer and the prospective buyer alike.

Off-plan sales gives the Developer peace of mind and allows their stock to byepass at-times saturated markets which in turn allows the developer to exit the development and move onto the next project quicker as we can usually exchange several months prior to practical completion. Instead of waiting just prior or post practical completion to market the new homes.

Off-plan sales is great for the new home buyer because it allows the new buyer to purchase their new home at todays price, instead of the price the new home will be worth in 1 or 2 years time.
