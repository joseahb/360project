SERVICES

360 APPROACH

The 360 approach to Buyers

Our key aim to our buyers is to provide a 360 service approach. We want to make sure that the road to purchasing your new home is as easy, exciting and enjoyable as it should be!

Mortgage Advice

Our trusted team of professional mortgage advisors will give you the best independent advice on your affordability ensuring you can purchase the right home for you. They will be able to run you through the current government schemes such as help-to-buy and find you the best mortgage available through their open panel network of lenders.

Solicitor Advice

Our panel of solicitors that work with 360 property are highly experienced in new homes transactions. They will be able to guide you through the process to ensure a smooth exchange and completion at an affordable cost.

Insurance Advice

Making sure your new home and its contents are secure is a key part of ownership. At 360 we are able to ensure this is possible through our network of helpful insurance brokers.

Early Access 

As we work closely with our network of residential property developers, we know when new homes are coming to the market before anyone else. Our early access allows you to have the first look at new homes before anyone else, so make sure you register your interest with us so you don't miss out on your future home. Because we work closely with several leading developers, we often have early access to new developments for our clients on our database. This allows our buyers the first chance look at developments to pick their favourite unit ahead of others and in certain circumstances, allow them design input into their new home.

The 360 approach to Developers

Development Valuation 

We pride ourselves on giving honest valuations on what we believe your development is worth. We use trialled and tested methods coupled with our market knowledge with software technology to ensure we provide accurate and reliable GDV figures.

Land Sourcing 

Using our advanced mapping software and always having our ears to the ground with our network of property experts, we source a variety of unconsented or consented sites for developers across London.
